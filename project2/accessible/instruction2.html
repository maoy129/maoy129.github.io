Red Bean Milk Pudding

红 豆 双 皮 奶

Milk Pudding:

◄◄ Back

  • STEP 1/7: Heat up milk to a light simmer and pour hot milk into a bowl for it to cool down under room temperature. ►►
  • STEP 2/7: Separate egg white from egg yolk. ►►
  • STEP 3/7: Poke a whole into the edge of the film that formed on top of the milk surface and carefully pour about 200ml of milk into a separate bowl. ►►
  • STEP 4/7: Mix milk, egg whites, and sugar. ►►
  • STEP 5/7: Pour mixture back from the edge of the original bowl. The film should remain floating on top. ►►
  • STEP 6/7: Steam on medium heat for 10 minutes. ►►
  • STEP 7/7: Refrigerate for 2 hours and put red beans on top.

    Done!
