  • Heat up milk to a light simmer and pour hot milk into a bowl for it to cool down under room temperature.
  • Separate egg white from egg yolk.
  • Poke a whole into the edge of the film that formed on top of the milk surface and carefully pour about 200ml of milk into a separate bowl.
  • Mix milk, egg whites, and sugar.
  • Pour mixture back from the edge of the original bowl. The film should remain floating on top.
  • Steam on medium heat for 10 minutes.
  • Refrigerate for 2 hours and put red beans on top.
