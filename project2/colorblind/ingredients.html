Red Bean Milk Pudding

红 豆 双 皮 奶

Ingredients:

Instruction:

    Sweet Red Beans
  • 150ml water
  • 50g red bean
  • 50g sugar
    Milk Pudding
  • 250ml milk
  • 1 egg white
  • 15g sugar
  1. Rinse red beans.
  2. Put red beans and water into pot.
    3. Boil without lid for 1 minute. Turn off heat, put on lid, and let it rest for 15 minutes.
  3. Repeat step 3 for 7 times. This makes sure that the outer shell of the red beans remain in shape.
  4. Turn on heat again and add sugar. Turn off heat when it boils. Drain the remaining liquid.
  5. Put beans into a container and let it rest over night.
  6. Heat up milk to a light simmer and pour hot milk into a bowl for it to cool down under room temperature.
  7. Separate egg white from egg yolk.
  8. Poke a whole into the edge of the film that formed on top of the milk surface and carefully pour about 200ml of milk into a separate bowl.
  9. Mix milk, egg whites, and sugar.
  10. Pour mixture back from the edge of the original bowl. The film should remain floating on top.
  11. Steam on medium heat for 10 minutes.
  12. Refrigerate for 2 hours and put red beans on top.
