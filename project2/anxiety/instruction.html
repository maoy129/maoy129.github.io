Red Bean Milk Pudding

红 豆 双 皮 奶

Sweet Red Beans:

  • Rinse red beans. ►►
  • Put red beans and water into pot. ►►
  • Boil without lid for 1 minute. Turn off heat, put on lid, and let it rest for 15 minutes. ►►
  • Repeat step 3 for 7 times. This makes sure that the outer shell of the red beans remain in shape. ►►
  • Turn on heat again and add sugar. Turn off heat when it boils. Drain the remaining liquid. ►►
  • Put beans into a container and let it rest over night.
